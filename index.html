Daffodil English Olympics 2021

It is needless to reiterate the importance of learning English in today’s world, but, unfortunately, very few people can show their proficiency in this language in Bangladesh. There is a lack of real motivation among students for improving this language, since mostly they treat it as a regular subject. So it is never too late to initiate something through which an additional motivation can be offered to the learners of Bangladesh to improve their English language skills. To do so, the Department of English of Daffodil International University is going to organize a competition of English language proficiency for students of class 9 to class 12 (including "O" and "A" level) from all over the country.

Rules of the Competition

Students of class 9 to 10 (as well as “O” level) and class 11 to 12 (as well as “A” level) can participate in the competition. Participants will have to pay ৳ 100 for completing registration. Selection round will be on online platform.

View Rules

Rules of the Competition

Registration
  • Participants from class 9 to 10 (or “O” level) are in Junior Category.
  • Participants from class 11 to 12 (or “A” level) are in Senior Category.
  • Pay ৳ 100 for registration confirmation and take part in the Selection Round.
  • There are three options for paying the registration fee:
    • bKash
    • On spot registration during the campaign in any school or college
    • Visiting the Department Office (Department of English, Daffodil International University, House 4/2, Level-05, Shobhanbag. Mirpur Road, Dhanmondi, Dhaka - 1207)
Selection Round (Online)
  • Access through Computer or Mobile Phone with active internet connection.
  • Any eligible candidate can take part in this round at any time from 25 October to 25 November 2019 from anywhere of the country.
  • The test will be on grammar, vocabulary, spelling, and an elementary knowledge of English literature.
  • There will be 40 multiple choice questions (MCQs) with four options for each.
  • Total time is 25 minutes.
  • Marks will vary depending on the time of submission.
  • Top 50 participants from each category will be selected for the Final Round.
Final Round
  • 13 December 2019 (Friday) in Auditorium 71 of Daffodil International University at 4/2, Sobhanbag, Mirpur Road, Dhanmondi, Dhaka - 1207.
